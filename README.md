<div>

  <h2>✨ Features</h2>

  <ul>

    <li>🐷 <strong>Piglin Bartering Weapons</strong>: Obtain unique custom weapons directly from Piglin trades</li>

    <li>⚔️ <strong>7 Unique Weapons</strong>: Each weapon has custom abilities, effects, particles and gameplay mechanics</li>

    <li>🧠 <strong>PDC System</strong>: Secure item recognition using PersistentDataContainer tags</li>

    <li>💥 <strong>Custom Combat Effects</strong>: Poison stacking, freeze effects, explosions, levitation and more</li>

    <li>🖥️ <strong>Optimized Listeners</strong>: Lightweight event-based system for high performance servers</li>

    <li>⚡ <strong>Balanced Drop Rates</strong>: Every weapon has its own rarity and chance</li>

  </ul>

</div>

<div>

  <h2>🎮 Weapons</h2>

  <details>

    <summary><strong>🔥 Arc du Nether Instable</strong></summary>

```yaml
Type: BOW
Rarity: Uncommon
Drop Rate: ~1.5%
```

- Explosive arrows
- Fire arrows
- Slowness arrows
- Random projectile effects
- Controlled explosions (no block damage)

  </details>

  <details>

    <summary><strong>☠️ Lame du Poison</strong></summary>

```yaml
Type: IRON_SWORD
Rarity: Common
Drop Rate: ~1.7%
```

- Poison stacks increase on every hit
- Stronger poison over time
- Stack reset after inactivity
- Heavy but deadly gameplay

  </details>

  <details>

    <summary><strong>🗡️ Dague du Voleur</strong></summary>

```yaml
Type: STONE_SWORD
Rarity: Uncommon
Drop Rate: ~1.2%
```

- Chance to steal enemy items
- Can steal directly to inventory
- Can force enemy drops on ground
- Works on players and mobs

  </details>

  <details>

    <summary><strong>⏳ Épée du Temps</strong></summary>

```yaml
Type: GOLDEN_SWORD
Rarity: Rare
Drop Rate: ~0.7%
```

- Applies Slowness and Mining Fatigue
- Freeze effect chance
- Portal/Snowflake particles
- Temporal control gameplay

  </details>

  <details>

    <summary><strong>🔥 Épée du Berserker</strong></summary>

```yaml
Type: DIAMOND_SWORD
Rarity: Rare
Drop Rate: ~0.8%
```

- Rage stacks system
- Increased speed and damage
- High-risk high-reward combat
- Weakness chance at max rage

  </details>

  <details>

    <summary><strong>👑 Épée du Jugement Piglin</strong></summary>

```yaml
Type: NETHERITE_SWORD
Rarity: Ultra Rare
Drop Rate: ~0.1%
```

- Damage scales with target gold
- Detects gold items in inventory
- Thunder sound effects
- One of the strongest weapons in the plugin

  </details>

  <details>

    <summary><strong>🎭 Bâton du Trickster</strong></summary>

```yaml
Type: BLAZE_ROD
Rarity: Common
Drop Rate: ~1.8%
```

- Completely random combat effects
- Poison, blindness, levitation and more
- Chaos-focused gameplay
- No cooldown between effects

  </details>

<div>

  <h2>⚙️ Commands</h2>

  <ul>

    <li><code>/pw reload</code> → Reload plugin configuration</li>

    <li><code>/pw give &lt;player&gt; &lt;weapon&gt;</code> → Give a custom weapon</li>

    <li><code>/pw list</code> → List all available weapons</li>

  </ul>

<div>

  <h2>🧠 Technical Details</h2>

  <ul>

    <li>✅ Uses <strong>PiglinBarterEvent</strong> for custom bartering</li>

    <li>✅ Uses <strong>PersistentDataContainer</strong> for weapon identification</li>

    <li>✅ Event listeners optimized for PvP and SMP servers</li>

    <li>✅ Cooldown systems to prevent spam and lag</li>

    <li>✅ Particle and sound integration for immersive combat</li>

  </ul>
